rockaz

We have been customers of this branch for over a decade. When we bought our home here, there was misinformation on the correct address. It was near the end of the year. All of the Christmas cards sent to us were returned "Undeliverable, address unknown" rather than inform us or simply deliver them with a notice stamp that the preferred address was different than written. Our delivery person is delightful. We can tell when she isn't working because we get a piece or two of the neighbors' mail, or like yesterday, our house simply gets skipped and the mail delivered the next day. Forget calling. I have spent over half an hour waiting for a live person to pick up, then have someone surly and terse, fail to even address my concerns. I've spoken to the branch manager (postmaster?) on more than one occasion. He seemed disinterested, burned out, and generally frustrated with the responsibilities of his job. I expect things will never change because it is a government "service." They don't have to respond to customer complaints or pressures from competition since the government has a monopoly on letter handling. Truly a shame, it would be something more resembling UPS or FedEx if only tax money wasn't poured in by power seeking bureaucrats.
