I was supposed to receive a package at my home, but I was notified that I could pick it up at the Carbondale post office. I tried to call them but no one answered on a Saturday a.m. So I made the 20-mile round trip grudgingly. I asked the clerk for my package and she went to get it out of the truck, which took a while. I appreciated her help and asked her why the box wasn’t delivered to my house. She said the the delivery person hadn’t shown up for work, and then she had quit. They are very short-staffed and they’re worried about what will happen at Christmas. No one wants to work. This clerk (maybe she’s the postmaster) was so nice, so patient, and so helpful. She’s definitely overworked. It ended up being a great trip to the PO because now I understand the problems they are having.
