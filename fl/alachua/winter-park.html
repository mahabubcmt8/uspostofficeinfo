Kudos USPS Alachua, Florida
1

This is not your father's Post Office. For years and years I have been a faithful UPS (Unbelievably Poor Service) customer but after attending a presentation on the ins and outs of USPS shipping for eBay customers I switch to USPS for all outgoing parcels.....(lots of them). The rates are better at USPS, the drivers and the staff are kinder and more interested in your success and if you have a problem ... they make it their problem until it gets resolved. Of course, 1st class mail is indeed that.... a First Class Service. If you are a FedEx Ground customer, or a UPS customer try it out, all the label printing, postage fees, payment options, tracking methods and handling can be done off your laptop or iPhone. I ditched FedEx when they back charged me $31.50 for a 12 pound parcel I shipped from Florida to Kentucky. Try it you'll be surprised how much better the rates are on small parcels........and how much nicer it is to work with people that live and work in the same town you do.
